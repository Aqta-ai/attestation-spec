# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple expectation: be respectful, assume good faith, and
focus criticism on ideas and code, not people.

## Expected

- Welcome newcomers and answer questions patiently.
- Keep technical disagreement precise and evidence-based.
- Respect privacy. Do not share others' personal data without consent.

## Not acceptable

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Publishing others' private information.
- Sustained disruption of discussion or review.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to **hello@aqta.ai**. Do not open a public
issue for interpersonal reports.

Maintainers may warn, restrict, or remove participants who violate this code.

This policy applies to GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any
official Aqta channels linked from this repository.
