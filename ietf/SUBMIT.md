## What this is and is not

An Internet-Draft is a dated public artefact, not a standard and not an
endorsement. Never say "IETF standard", "submitted to IETF for
approval", or "IETF-backed". Honest wording: "an Internet-Draft
documenting the deployed format, submitted to the independent stream".
